What We Study

We use circuit-level approaches to understand the brain mechanisms of reinforcement in health and disease.

01

Reinforcement circuits

We dissect how lateral hypothalamus (LHA) neurons and dopamine (DA) signaling mediate positive and negative reinforcement across the striatum.

02

Substance use disorder

We study how reinforcement circuits are recruited and transformed during the development and expression of opioid and drug motivation.

03

Neural imaging & behavior

We combine 2-photon calcium imaging, multi-site fiber photometry, and head-fixed behavioral paradigms to link circuit activity to behavior.

Techniques

2-photon calcium imaging Multi-site fiber photometry Optogenetics Chemogenetics (DREADDs) Head-fixed behavior Intravenous self-administration Holographic optogenetics Generalized linear models Machine learning Open-source hardware
