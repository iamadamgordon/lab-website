Join the Lab

The Gordon Lab will open at Rutgers University in Fall 2026. We are looking for driven, curious researchers who want to understand how the brain mediates reinforcement and motivation. Reach out at ag3078@psych.rutgers.edu.

Graduate Students

We welcome applications from prospective Ph.D. students interested in circuit neuroscience, motivated behavior, and substance use disorder. Students will gain expertise in cutting-edge approaches including 2-photon calcium imaging, multi-site fiber photometry, optogenetics, and head-fixed behavioral paradigms.

Please apply through the Rutgers University Psychology Ph.D. program (Behavioral and Systems Neuroscience area). Mention your interest in the Gordon Lab in your application. Before applying, feel free to reach out by email with your CV and a brief description of your research interests.

Postdoctoral Fellows

We are actively recruiting postdoctoral fellows with experience in in-vivo electrophysiology, calcium imaging, fiber photometry, optogenetics, or behavioral neuroscience. A strong quantitative background is a plus. We offer a collaborative and rigorous scientific environment with excellent resources.

To apply, please send: (1) a CV, (2) a brief statement of your research interests and why you want to join the lab, and (3) contact information for 2–3 references.

Research Technicians

We periodically recruit research technicians to assist with animal behavior, histology, data collection, and day-to-day lab operations. If you are interested in a technician position, please email with your CV and a brief statement of interest.

Undergraduate Researchers

We welcome undergraduates at Rutgers who are passionate about neuroscience and want hands-on research experience. Please email a short description of your interests and your current year and major.
