We are thrilled to announce that the Gordon Lab will open in the **Department of Psychology at Rutgers University** starting Fall 2026.

Our lab studies the neural circuit mechanisms of motivated behaviors, with a particular focus on lateral hypothalamus (LHA) circuits and their regulation of striatal dopamine signaling. We are interested in how these circuits are recruited and transformed throughout the development of substance use disorder.

We are **actively recruiting** at all levels:

- **Graduate students**
- **Postdoctoral fellows**
- **Research technicians**
