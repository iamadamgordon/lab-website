2025 · Neuron

Lateral hypothalamic glutamate and GABA neurons cooperatively shape striatum-wide dopamine dynamics during consumption

Gordon-Fennell A., Benowitz B.M., Barbakh J.M., Montequin I., Campuzano A., Stevenson H., Hjort M.M., Critz M., Ancell E., Witten D.M., Stuber G.D.

In revision at Neuron — First author

The lateral hypothalamus (LHA) is a critical hub for motivated behavior, but how GABA and glutamate subpopulations cooperatively regulate dopamine (DA) dynamics across the striatum to shape consumption remains unclear. We used multi-site fiber photometry to map DA release across anterior–posterior striatal subregions during consummatory behavior and determined the causal role of LHA GABA and glutamate neurons in shaping these dynamics.

2023 · eLife

An open-source platform for head-fixed operant and consummatory behavior

Gordon-Fennell A., Barbakh J.M., Utley M., Singh S., Bazzino P., Gowrishankar R., Bruchas M.R., Roitman M.F., Stuber G.D.

We developed OHRBETS (Open-source Head-fixed Rodent Behavioral and Electrophysiology Testing System), a low-cost, open-source platform for measuring operant and consummatory behaviors in head-fixed mice. OHRBETS enables a range of behavioral paradigms — including instrumental conditioning for sucrose, brief access taste tasks, intracranial self-stimulation, and real-time place preference — while head-fixing animals for compatibility with calcium imaging, fiber photometry, and electrophysiology. Validated across multiple labs and freely available.

2021 · Neuropharmacology

Illuminating subcortical GABAergic and glutamatergic circuits for reward and aversion

Gordon-Fennell A., Stuber G.D.

This review synthesizes the functional roles of subcortical GABA and glutamate neurons in mediating reward and aversive valence. We discuss how opposing inhibitory and excitatory subpopulations in structures including the lateral hypothalamus, ventral tegmental area, and extended amygdala shape motivated behavior, with implications for understanding the neural basis of reinforcement.
